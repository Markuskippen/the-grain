ABOUT THE GRAIN



The grain is a memory recording neural implant device that will record everything you see through your eyes,
allowing you to look back at your memories at any time.

You will be able to look back at all of your memories,
either in your head or on screen. We call this process a “redo”.





With the grain, comes a small,
handheld console which
allows you to use the menu.

This can also be used for
contactless payments in the future.






The product is still under development,
but we are aiming to do the first insertions in 2025.

The procedure will only take 20 minutes using local anesthetic.
For more information, please contact us.




Warning: removing the Grain without without the aid of medical proffesionals is dangerous and may cause permanent brain and eye damage. Please consult with your doctor before doing the procedure. Maimar INC
